Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how CalcHub handles information when you use our calculators and pages.

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Information We Collect

CalcHub is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly. The numbers you enter into calculators are processed in your browser and are not intentionally sent to our server. We do not ask you to create an account, submit passwords or provide sensitive personal information to use the calculators.

Cookies And Analytics

We may use basic analytics tools to understand page visits, device types, browser information and general usage trends. These tools help us improve page speed, calculator quality and content. Analytics data is generally aggregated and does not directly identify you.

Advertising

CalcHub may display ads through Google AdSense or other advertising partners. Advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings and through ad personalization options provided by advertising networks.

Third-Party Links

Our website may link to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content or policies of third-party sites. Please review their policies before sharing information with them.

Data Security

We try to keep the site safe and avoid collecting unnecessary information. However, no website or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

CalcHub is a general calculator website. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review it.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through the Contact Us page.
